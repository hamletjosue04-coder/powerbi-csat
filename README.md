# powerbi-csat
Power BI Portfolio Project about CSAT Performance on BPO

📌 Project Overview

Developed a Power BI dashboard to analyze Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) performance and identify key drivers impacting customer experience.

The project includes KPI tracking, customer segmentation, root cause analysis, key influencers, and actionable recommendations to improve operational performance.

The dataset was created based on my experience working in the BPO industry and was designed to simulate real-world customer service and operational scenarios.

🎯 Business Problem

A BPO organization has failed to meet its CSAT target for three consecutive months, creating operational risk and increasing client dissatisfaction.

The objective of this project was to identify the operational drivers behind the decline and provide data-driven recommendations to recover performance.

📈 Dashboard Features

CSAT Performance vs Target
Customer Tenure Analysis
CSAT Drivers Analysis
Agent Performance Analysis
Key Influencers
Root Cause Investigation
Business Recommendations

🔍 Key Findings

CSAT has remained below target for three consecutive months.
Loyal customers contributed the largest impact of low CSAT.
Technical issues represented the most impactful category.
Agent-related factors (controllable) were responsible for most negative customer experiences.
Multiple transfers and lack of ownership were operational drivers

💡 Business Recommendations

Reduce unnecessary call transfers.
Provide ownership training to agents.
Prioritize coaching for high-impact agents.
Track CSAT improvement through weekly operational reviews.
